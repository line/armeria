# Virtual office hours

Armeria virtual office hours are informal Google Meet sessions anyone can join twice a week to talk about
any topics related with Armeria, including but not limited to:

* Questions
  * "Can I use Armeria for *&lt;X&gt;*?"
  * "How does Armeria compare to *&lt;Y&gt;*?"
  * "How do I do *&lt;Z&gt;* with Armeria?"
* Contribution
  * "How does Armeria work internally?"
  * "I want to contribute to Armeria. Where should I start?"
* Project future
  * "What's the next cool feature we should work on?"
  * "What should we fix or improve?"
* Just getting familiar with community members

## How to join

* Check [the calendar](#calendar) to find the right time.
  * Note that all events are shown in UTC.
* Join [our Google Meet session](https://meet.google.com/bha-ygps-zmm) on time.

<Warning>

* Mind [virtual meeting etiquette](https://www.goto.com/blog/7-rules-virtual-meeting-etiquette-every-professional-know).
* We expect all office hour participants to follow [our code of conduct](/community/code-of-conduct).

</Warning>
